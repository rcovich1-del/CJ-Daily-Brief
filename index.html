Strategy in Focus
Bullish · Undefined Risk Credit

Cash-Secured Put

Sell a put against cash collateral to collect premium, taking on the obligation to buy the stock at the strike if assigned.

Best when implied volatility is elevated and you'd be happy to own the underlying at a lower, discounted entry.

DTE: 30–45 DTE IV Rank: > 50% preferred CSP

Why Tonight

After a ~40% VIX spike, put premiums are rich — exactly the backdrop cash-secured puts are built for. With the regime in caution rather than outright defense, selectively selling puts on names you'd genuinely own lets you get paid to set discounted entries. Tonight's discipline is patience and small size: sell into strength, not into the gap.

VIX 18.92 (+40%) — elevated premium, but sub-20 keeps this a caution, not crisis, environment.

SPY back below its 20-day yet holding the 50-day; QQQ -4.8% — respect the downtrend, favor patience.
