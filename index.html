Long Call

Buy a single call option to profit from a directional move higher, with maximum risk limited to the premium paid.

Best when you are directionally bullish on a stock and implied volatility is historically low — reducing the premium outlay while maintaining full upside exposure.

DTE: 30–45 DTE IV Rank: IV Rank < 50% preferred LC

Why Tonight

The Normal regime (VIX 15.77, SPY above 20D SMA by 2.19%) supports offensive debit plays on Tuesday's rotation schedule. Today's AI chip rally — MRVL +32%, HPE +19% — confirms momentum in tech and AI-adjacent names.
