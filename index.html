Strategy in Focus

Cash Secured Put CSP BULLISH
Undefined-downside credit (cash-secured) · 30–45 DTE · IV Rank > 40% preferred
Sell an out-of-the-money put and hold cash to buy the stock if assigned — you get paid to set a lower buy price. Best when IV is elevated and you'd genuinely welcome owning the name lower.
Why tonight: VIX jumped +12% to 18.65 and IV Ranks are rich across the board, but breadth flipped Risk-Off and SPY is sitting on its 50-day. That mix argues against directional debit risk and for getting paid to wait — sell puts only on names you'd happily own on a further pullback, cash-secured, well below support.
