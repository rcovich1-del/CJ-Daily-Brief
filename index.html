HIGH

HIGH

Rotation Into Financials & Health
MEDIUM

MEDIUM

Strategy in Focus
Bullish · Undefined Risk Credit

Cash-Secured Put

Sell a put and set aside the cash to buy the stock at the strike — collecting premium while agreeing to own shares at a discount if assigned.

Best in elevated IV Rank on a quality name you'd happily own, with earnings cleared and price holding above key support.

DTE: 30–45 DTE IV Rank: IV Rank > 40% preferred CSP

Why Tonight

Thursday's rotation slot in a Normal regime is the CSP. With the S&P above its 20-day, breadth broadening (68% of names above their 20-day) and the VIX subdued, selling downside premium on strong names is well-supported — provided you steer clear of the semiconductor wreck.

VIX 16.15 (+0.9%) with VXST 12.4 < VXV 19.0 < VXMT 21.5 — calm, normal contango; near-term fear is low, so pick spots rather than force size.

SPY $744.78 sits above its 20/50/200-day averages even as QQQ slips below its 20-day — a bifurcated tape rewarding stock selection over index beta.
