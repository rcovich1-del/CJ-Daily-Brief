Strategy in Focus
Bullish · Defined Risk Debit

Ratio Call Diagonal

Buy a longer-dated call and sell a shorter-dated, higher-strike call (often in a ratio) to finance upside exposure while collecting front-month decay.

Best in a steady, low-to-moderate-volatility uptrend where the underlying grinds higher toward the short strike.

DTE: Front 30-45 DTE / Back 60-90 DTE IV Rank: IV Rank 40-60% preferred RCD

Why Tonight

With the desk in a caution regime and tech leadership cracking, directional long exposure is risky - but money is rotating into real estate, staples and energy. A bullish diagonal on a low-beta REIT leader keeps directional risk small and defined while the broad tape chops, letting the position profit from a slow grind higher rather than a sharp move.

VIX 18.77 (+6.8%) - moderate zone; near-term vol elevated but the term structure remains in contango (+5.4).

SPY -0.54% / QQQ -1.64% with tech breaking, but real estate led (+2.1%) - favor low-beta bullish structures over outright longs.
