Strategy in Focus
Bullish · Defined Risk Debit

Ratio Call Diagonal

Buy a longer-dated, deeper-in-the-money call and sell a shorter-dated, further out-of-the-money call (often more than one) to finance it - a bullish position with reduced cost basis.

Best on a relative-strength name with a moderate bullish drift and front-month IV rich enough to sell against the long call.

DTE: Back 45-60 DTE / Front 15-30 DTE IV Rank: IV Rank 40-70% preferred RCD

Why Tonight

With VIX at 18.4 in the caution band and Health Care the clear leadership group (+7.9% on the week), a Ratio Call Diagonal lets the desk lean bullish on a relative-strength name while financing the long call with rich front-month premium. The contango term structure (VXST 16.8 < VXMT 22.3) favors collecting elevated near-dated IV rather than paying up for it.

VIX 18.4 - moderate: front-month IV is rich enough to sell, not so high it signals panic.

SPY is churning just below its 20- and 50-day; QQQ rose +0.8% Friday but -4.6% on the week. Lean on sector relative strength, not the index.
