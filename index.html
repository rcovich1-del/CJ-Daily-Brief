Strategy in Focus
Bullish · Defined Risk Debit

Long Call

Buying a call gives leveraged upside exposure to a stock with risk capped at the premium paid.

Best when you have bullish directional conviction and implied volatility is low, so the option premium is cheap.

DTE: 30–45 DTE IV Rank: IV Rank < 50% preferred LC

Why Tonight

With VIX at 16.50 and SPY still above its 20- and 50-day averages, the primary trend is intact even as tech corrects. The day's leadership rotated into financials on strong bank earnings, and low-IV financial names offer cheap call premium. Long calls let us press that value/financials rotation with strictly defined risk rather than paying up for elevated put premium.

VIX 16.50 (+14%) — near-term vol popped but stayed in the moderate zone, keeping call premium reasonable.

SPY +0.9% above its 20-day and financials (XLF) leading, while QQQ lags as megacap tech unwinds.
