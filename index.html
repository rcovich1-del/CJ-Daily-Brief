Strategy in Focus
Bullish · Undefined Risk Credit

Cash-Secured Put

Sell a put and set aside cash to buy the stock at the strike, collecting premium while you wait.

Best on a stock you'd happily own, in elevated IV, with earnings cleared before expiration.

DTE: 30-45 DTE IV Rank: IV Rank > 40% preferred CSP

Why Tonight

With VIX at 16.73 and the S&P holding above its rising 20-day, the tape is Normal and constructive - ideal for collecting premium rather than paying for it. Today's rotation into financials, staples and energy rewards selling puts on quality names in strong groups. A cash-secured put lets the desk get paid to set bids under stocks it would own on a dip.

VIX 16.73 (-5%); the 9-day (13.98) sits below the 3-month (19.50) - no near-term stress, and premium is still adequate.

SPY +0.40% above its 20- and 50-day; QQQ -0.27% as tech lags - favor put-selling in non-tech leaders.

  • Set a management plan: take profit near 50% or roll at 21 DTE; accept assignment only at the discount you want.
